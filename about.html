About

Ivan Ho is an aspiring software engineer based in Monterey Park, CA, with a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Communications and a minor in Computer Science from California State University, Los Angeles. He has a strong academic background with a GPA of 3.55 and relevant coursework in relational databases, programming, and data structures.

Ivan has developed a diverse skill set in various programming languages, including Java, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C, XML, and Kotlin. He is proficient in using essential computer applications such as Android Studio, Microsoft Excel, Salesforce, Git, and GitHub, and holds certifications in Android Development from CodePath and Python Basics for Data Science from AI Planet.

In his professional journey, Ivan has gained valuable experience through various roles. As a Software Engineering Fellow at Headstarter AI, he led a team in project management and adapted to changes to meet goals effectively. During his tenure as a Student Trainee in Engineering at the United States Army DEVCOM C5ISR, he participated in Agile ceremonies, led component testing within Salesforce, and utilized project management tools to enhance product quality. Additionally, his role as a Software Engineering Intern at National Builders Control involved debugging code, problem-solving, and effective communication with department heads.

Ivan's leadership abilities were showcased during his time as the Innovation Committee Leader for the Association of Computing Machinery at CSULA, where he collaborated with a team to develop a mobile application for club updates and coordinated recruitment and project management initiatives.

He has also worked on various projects, including developing a job search app, creating a community-leader intermediary website, and designing a Java program resembling the Wordle game. Through these projects, Ivan has demonstrated his skills in UX/UI principles, front-end development, object-oriented programming, and effective team communication.

Ivan is a dedicated and adaptable professional, eager to leverage his skills and experiences to contribute to innovative and impactful software engineering projects.
